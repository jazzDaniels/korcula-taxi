What do we offer?

We offer you comfortable taxi service to your accommodation, to the most beautiful island Korčula beaches (Lumbarda, Vaja, Pupnatska luka, Žitna, Vela Luka, Proizd Island...) and to the popular restaurants. Also, we offer transfer to the wineries on the island of Korčula and the peninsula of Pelješac. Please contact us and book the taxi service to your destination by our passenger car or for a group if required.

- Mario Didović, CEO

About us

We provide fast and highly professional taxi services on the island of Korčula, Croatia. Taxi service „Mario“ offer you affordable prices in the area of Dalmatia. Our aim is to deliver you unforgettable travel experiences. For all taxi services on the island of Korčula, we are at your service 24 hours a day. Our service is available all year long for any transfers to the airports Dubrovnik and Split as well.
